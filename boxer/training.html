Training Plan (Age 12–15)
Follow each card carefully. Focus on technique and safety over speed or power.
Skipping — Coordination & Warm-up
6 rounds × 30 seconds (30 sec rest)
  1. Stand tall, elbows close to ribs.
  2. Turn the rope using wrists only.
  3. Jump lightly (small jumps only).
  4. Round 1–2: two feet.
  5. Round 3–4: alternate feet.
  6. Round 5–6: best form, relaxed pace.
Safety: Stop if ankles or shins hurt. Train on a clear, non-slip surface.
Shadowboxing — Technique
4 rounds × 2 minutes (1 min rest)
  1. Hands up, chin down, knees soft.
  2. Round 1: movement only.
  3. Round 2: jab + movement.
  4. Round 3: jab–cross, reset guard.
  5. Round 4: add light slip after punches.
Safety: Keep distance from walls/furniture. Stop if dizzy.
Mobility & Injury Prevention
  1. Ankle circles × 10 each way.
  2. Calf raises × 2 sets of 10.
  3. Bodyweight squats × 8.
  4. Shoulder circles × 10.
  5. Slow breathing × 60 sec.
Safety: No pain. Move slowly and with control.
